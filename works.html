STAFF-DATA

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Just like the name ....Staff-Data..., this App has pre-stored information and data on each individual of a registered company or organisation. The scope behind this website is to provide a platform that has the electronic copies of certification, data and identification of every individual working for an organization registered to use the App. The whole idea is to surpass the ancient book-keeping method of staff record. The default staff id to be use in the demo of this App is 001 to 006
